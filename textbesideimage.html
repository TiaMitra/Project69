Beach Bites Canteen is owned and operated by the MVPS P and C as a service to the families of Manly Village Public School. Canteen Manager Lori and her staff, Diane and Attracta aim to provide fresh, healthy and affordable food on a daily basis to all Manly Village children.The canteen provides a uniqueopportunity for children to interact with different adults, use money and also practice patience and use their beautiful manners during their transactions.The canteen prides itself on providing support to all children everyday.
